    About Flashy

    Flashy is a free, open-source math game designed to help kids practice arithmetic in a fun, engaging way.

    Players guide Flashy the dog through 4 worlds, each focusing on a different operation:

    • Addition Alps
    • Subtraction Swamp
    • Multiplication Meadows
    • Division Desert

    The game features 40 levels of increasing difficulty, friendly characters to meet, and boss battles to conquer!

    Open Source

    Flashy is released under the MIT License. The source code is available on GitHub:

    github.com/Ported/flashy

    Contributions, bug reports, and feedback are welcome!

    Technology

    Built with Python and Pyodide, running entirely in your browser. Hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: November 2025

    What We Collect

    Flashy collects only your chosen player name for the leaderboard. That's it.

    • No email addresses
    • No personal information
    • No tracking or analytics
    • No cookies

    Local Storage

    Your game progress is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

    Leaderboard

    When you create a player, your chosen name and score are stored on our server (Cloudflare D1) and displayed on the public leaderboard. No other data is collected.

    No Commercial Use

    Flashy is a non-commercial, open-source educational project. We don't sell data, show ads, or have any commercial objectives.
